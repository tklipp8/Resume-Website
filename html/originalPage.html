Pizza

Pizza is one of my favorite foods, and I know how to order it well. Some people just go for whatever the restaurant gives you. Plain cheese? Are you even trying? Let's spice up that pizza order of yours. The key to a great pizza is changing how much of each ingredient is put on the pizza (most chains let you change how much cheese, sauce, or other ingredients you can put on your pizza). The key to a truly great pizza is to add less cheese. So many pizzas add too much cheese. If your at a chain, the cheese is cheaper and is pretty nasty. Don't even think about ordering extra cheese. The key lies in the sauce.

What is a pizza without its sauce? A sad cheesy bread that no one wants. Add more sauce, cause most chains do not add enough. After this, go crazy with your ingredients. Pepperoni is a classic, but consider trying: peppers, mushrooms, sausage, and any amount of other ingredients will make your pizza experience pleasant and worth it. If you order pineapple on pizza, abandon hope because no amount of advice in this mortal coil can save your pizza now. May the Little Cesar give merciful judgement for you pineapple sins.


To recap:

  1. Less cheese is a must!
  2. More sauce is necessary!
  3. Try new and different ingredients, like:
    • Peppers
    • Mushrooms
    • Sausage
    • Anything else to spice up that pizza!
  4. Pineapple on pizza is a gateway to many other dangerous things, like dipping fries in a milkshake, or putting sprinkles on cheese.
